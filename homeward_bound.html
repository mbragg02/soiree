Homeward Bound...

This August, Chairperson Clachan Shanda will be returning to his native Scotland to take part in the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.

Mr Shanda will be appearing in a personal capacity, independent of his official role as Society Chair.  He will be collaborating with a non-society member, on a 45-minute revelatory exposé event titled: ‘Peter Piper – The Man Behind The Legend.’

Dates: 5th - 26th August

Venue: 203, Princes Mall, Space L8 (downstairs)

Time: 16:45 (45mins)

Tickets: This show is free and non-ticketed, just arrive

For further details about content, venue and show times please visit www.edfringe.com after the 31st May.
