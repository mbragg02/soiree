Join the SOIREE

First read the manifesto and society rules to make sure they align at least loosely with your own beliefs and ideology.

If after reading the relevant information you feel you would like to come along to a meeting or be part of a future event, please send an email to:

thechairperson@thesoiree.org.uk

Give your email the subject - ‘MEMBERSHIP’ and include your name, telephone number and details of your own specific area of historical interest.

Book the SOIREE

If you would like to book The SOIREE to perform at an event, please send an email to:

thechairperson@thesoiree.org.uk

Give your email the subject – ‘BOOKING’ and include your name, telephone number, a description of the event including dates, timings, venue and any other relevant information.
